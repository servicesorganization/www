What is Orange Workspace Services?

Orange Workspace Services is an managed ecosystem of combined devices and services that empowers employees to do their work, anytime, anywhere, in the most productive, user-friendly and secure way.

Our Workspace Services capabilities will:

1: Support hybrid work scenarios so you can consume your digital workspace anytime, anywhere and on any device, empower collaboration and productivity and provide secure access to your companies applications and data.

2: Boost employee experience by delivering a unified employee experience across all devices, ensure the best possible digital workspace performance and include an adaptive workspace portal boosting the employee experience and productivity.

3: Simplify complexity by using market standard PaaS and SaaS cloud services as the foundation, supports a smooth transition from legacy to SaaS applications without bothering the employees and supports continuous innovations within the Workspace Services.

Standard Services and Modules

Our Workspace Services are based on standard service components which have been developed based on our extensive knowledge and experience, all components are designed to meet the customer demands in a secure way.

Easy to Configure and Implement

All services exist out of predefined modules and because of this modular approach, we can quickly configure and implement the services.
